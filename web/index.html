DartCrypto

Designed and coded by Ilya Bardinov

Follow @ilya_bardinov on Twitter

DartCrypto is an OpenSource web application under the Apache License where you can convert, encode and decode, encrypt and decrypt content, messages, text between different format systems and by different algorithms. This happens fully in your browser using JavaScript, compiled from Dart, no content will be sent to any kind of server. Any feedback appreciated, just leave me a tweet, message or contribute.

Attention! Some encryption methods offered below are very basic and therefore not considered as secure.
